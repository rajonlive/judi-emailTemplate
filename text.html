Here's the thing about human beings in general

WE'RE NEVER READY — we’re never ready to break through to new horizons, to see hope beyond hopelessness nor resounding courage beyond fear. Take our NYE resolutions that in essence turn out to be merely wishful thinking; those once a year promises we make at midnight to HOPEFULLY change the things about ourselves that disappoint us the most.

WE RESIST CHANGE OF ANY TYPE — and most often to our own detriment. Out of FEAR we refuse to acknowledge CHANGE as being a builder of inner strength, emotional resilience and growth on so many levels.

WE RELY ON COMFORT — Those deeply impregnated patterns of behaviour that in most instances provide only temporary and feel-good relief rather than any serious commitment on our part to long term change.

WE STRUGGLE WITH HONESTY — being really honest with ourselves is overwhelming, so confronting and absolutely detrimental to the stability of our emotional walls of defence or armour plating if you will. Yet, it is those seemingly impenetrable walls of protection that need to tumble one by one for real and lasting change to be effected.
